Why Blind Skateboarders Are Amazing

Skateboarding without sight isn’t just impressive — it’s revolutionary. Blind skateboarders rely on sound, touch, and instinct to perform incredible tricks and inspire the world.

Icons like Justin Bishop and Dan Mancina prove that creativity has no limits. Their accomplishments push adaptive sports forward.
